Who we are

A pizzeria, not a chain.

Seventy-one seats in the dining room. Private parties on the quieter nights. Delivery within a ten-minute drive. No app, no loyalty program, no upsell screen at the counter — just hand-tossed crust, sauce we cook ourselves, and twenty-two years of regulars who'd notice if we changed it.
