## 2. Confirm the menu (10 minutes)

Most existing sites have **wrong prices** because they haven't been updated in a year or two. Walk through every category. Cross out, write in the new price.

- [ ] Pizza (small / large / sicilian / specialty)
- [ ] Calzones / strombolis
- [ ] Subs / heroes / steaks
- [ ] Salads
- [ ] Sides / munchies
- [ ] Dinners / pasta
- [ ] Desserts
- [ ] Drinks (if listed)
- [ ] **Anything they ADDED or DROPPED** since the existing site was built

Bring a printout of the existing menu. Mark it up live.

---

## 3. Grab the photos (15 minutes)

You take all of these with your phone. Decent natural light only — turn off the kitchen overheads if they're harsh.

**Mandatory (5):**
- [ ] **Hero food shot** — their best-selling dish, top-down, on a clean surface
- [ ] **Storefront** — wide, daytime, no people walking through the frame
- [ ] **Interior** — wide shot of the dining room with one table set
- [ ] **Action** — owner or cook hand-tossing dough OR plating a dish
- [ ] **Owner** — a real photo of the owner or family, smiling, not stiff

**Bonus (3-5 more if time):**
- [ ] One more food shot (a different dish)
- [ ] One detail shot (a slice being lifted, sauce being poured, etc.)
- [ ] Bar / counter / register area if it has character

**On the spot:** AirDrop or email the photos to yourself before you leave. Don't trust phone storage.

---

## 4. The integration choice (5 minutes)

Ask: **"Do you take online orders right now?"**

| Their answer | What you do |
|---|---|
| "Yes, we use [Toast / Slice / ChowNow / DoorDash]" | Get the link. Wire the "Order online" button to it. |
| "No, just phone" | Recommend Toast Online Ordering ($69/mo, takes 30 min to set up). Quote it as a separate add-on ($300 setup + their Toast fee). |
| "I don't want online ordering" | Wire the CTA to the `tel:` link. Note: they're leaving money on the table, but their call. |

- [ ] **Choice:** _______________________________________
- [ ] **Link or vendor:** _______________________________

## 5. The hosting + domain decision (5 minutes)

Most restaurant owners don't know what these are. Explain plainly.

> "Your website lives on a computer somewhere. That computer charges rent — about $20 a month. Your domain (the .com name) is separate — about $15 a year."

- [ ] **Existing domain:** ____________________________
- [ ] **Keep it?** ☐ Yes  ☐ Buy new one (which: ___________________)
- [ ] **Hosting:** ☐ Vercel free year then $20/mo  ☐ Their existing host (which: _____________)
- [ ] **Who pays Vercel/host after year 1:** ☐ Owner directly  ☐ JJ retainer

## 7. The reviews + testimonials question (3 minutes)

Existing site has Brandon M and Jen H. Confirm those are real and they're okay using them.

- [ ] **Brandon M — real? Permission to keep?** ☐ Yes  ☐ Replace
- [ ] **Jen H — real? Permission to keep?** ☐ Yes  ☐ Replace
- [ ] **Want a fresh review section** (pulled live from Google)? ☐ Yes  ☐ No
- [ ] **Other regulars who'd give a quote** (get name + line): _______________

---

## 8. Permission + payment (5 minutes)

Get these answers BEFORE you start building. Write them down so there's no confusion later.

- [ ] **Can JJ use this site on his portfolio + show it to other restaurants?** ☐ Yes  ☐ Yes after 30 days  ☐ No
- [ ] **Permission to take credit in the footer** ("Site by JJ")? ☐ Yes  ☐ No
- [ ] **Deposit** ($________ ) — how paying:  ☐ Stripe link now  ☐ Cash today  ☐ Check
- [ ] **Final** — how paying:  ☐ Stripe on go-live  ☐ Cash on go-live  ☐ Net 7 invoice

---

## 9. The "weird thing" question (2 minutes)

Always ask, in this exact phrasing:

> "Is there anything weird about your business I should know? Like — a section the old site doesn't have but should? A thing customers always ask you that the menu doesn't answer? A wrong fact on the current site that drives you crazy?"

Write down their answer. It usually unlocks the one section that makes the new site feel like THEM:

## 10. Confirm the install timeline (1 minute)

Repeat back to the owner what they bought, when, for how much:

> "Okay — I'm going to have your new site live by [____pm on ___day]. It'll cost $______ total. I'm taking the deposit now. I'll send you a preview link by [____pm], you click around, tell me yes or change-this-one-thing, then I push live. Sound good?"

- [ ] **Owner confirmed:** ☐ Yes  ☐ Sleep on it (follow up _____________)

---

## After you leave

- [ ] **Same day:** Email the owner a recap of everything above, signed by you. PDF or plain text.
- [ ] **Same day:** Send the Stripe deposit link.
- [ ] **Day of install:** Text the owner at 9am: "On my way at 11."
- [ ] **Day of go-live:** Text the owner when the site is live. Include the URL.
- [ ] **3 days after go-live:** Check in. "Anything you want changed?"
- [ ] **2 weeks after go-live:** Ask for a referral. "Know any other restaurants on the Shore that need this?"
