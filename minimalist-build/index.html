Who we are

A pizzeria, not a chain.

DePietro's opened on Milford Street in 2004. We make New York-style pizza the way the family learned it — hand-tossed crust, sauce we cook ourselves, mozzarella that didn't come pre-shredded.

Seventy-one seats in the dining room. Private parties on the quieter nights. Delivery within a ten-minute drive. No app, no loyalty program, no upsell screen at the counter.

"Simply the best pizza in Salisbury. I've been ordering from DePietro's since they opened and I love their pizza." — Brandon M, Salisbury
